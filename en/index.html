October 04, 2019

MoBike - Riding from China to Italy

Mobike is the world's biggest bicycle-sharing operator. The Chinese company, headquartered in Beijing, was launched in Italy by Evlonet Group, which is specialized in Cross Border E-Commerce. Alessandro Felici, the company's founder and CEO, will present Evlonet Group with a focus on Mobike's history, its strategy in Italy and in Europe, the spread and the potential of Sharing Economy. Dragon Boat Party

Have you ever heard about Dragon Boat Festival? Don't miss the Dragon Boat Party to celebrate this important Chinese festivity together!
